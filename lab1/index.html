The Runaway Cat

The Background

The classic children book "The RUNAWAY BUNNY" by Margeret Wise Brown and Pictures by Clement Hurd that has extremely simple plot (it is a children book after all):

A small rabbit wants to run away but his mother tells him that "if you run away, I will run after you..."

But our version is a bit different, instead of bunny, it's CAT in our version :) You can navigate the story by clicking left and right arrows.

Prelude

They stay in one of the cell in the grid - their home, labeled as H in the grid.There are lots of obstacles (at least 4 cells) in the 2D grid world, labeled as # in the grid. The small cat wants to explore this 2D grid world with his mother cat.
